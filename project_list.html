The Rise and Decline of North Lawndale: how industrial history shaped a neighborhood

Date completed: October, 2020

Illinois Geographer published this research paper on the history of North Lawndale in their Winter 2021 edition. The paper summarizes how twentieth century industrial history led to economic hardship and racial tension in the Rust Belt neighborhood of North Lawndale. The paper breaks North Lawndaleâ€™s history into four eras spanning from the mid-nineteenth century to the present. The first three era depict how changes to urban infrastructure and industrial operations led to the rise and decline of North Lawndale. The final era highlights revitalization efforts that have helped the neighborhood recover from deindustrialization.
